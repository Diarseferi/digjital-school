Real Madrid, founded in 1902, is one of the most successful and iconic football clubs in the world. Based in Madrid, Spain, it has become synonymous with excellence, both on and off the field. With a rich history spanning over a century, the club's success in domestic and international football has solidified its place as a global powerhouse. Real Madrid's legacy is built not only on its numerous trophies and accolades but also on its unmatched ability to nurture talent, influence football culture, and maintain a global fan base.
